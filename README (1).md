## What is Estary?

Estary is a property management platform built from scratch for Filipino landlords. It handles rent reminders, GCash & Maya collection, tenant records, and BIR-ready reports — all in one place.

Hindi ito foreign app na basta-basta na-translate. Built specifically for the **Philippine rental market** — para sa Pinoy landlords, ng Pinoy landlords.

---

## Features

- 🤖 &nbsp;**AI Rent Reminders** — Personalized SMS reminders per tenant, in Filipino or English, with a one-tap GCash payment link
- 💙 &nbsp;**GCash & Maya Collection** — Tenants pay using apps already on their phone. No new app needed.
- 📊 &nbsp;**BIR-Ready Reports** — Income & expense reports formatted for Philippine rental tax requirements
- 📋 &nbsp;**Lease Management** — Store and track lease agreements. Get notified before renewals.
- 🔔 &nbsp;**Overdue Tracking** — Know exactly who owes what, automatically
- 🏠 &nbsp;**Multi-Property Support** — Manage apartments, condos, bedspace, and transient units
